My Fabulous Vacation

Location: Glacier National Park

Recently, I went on a vacation to Glacier National Park in Montana.

The plan was to do a 10 day long hiking/camping trip. With the potential to encounter:

  • Bears
  • Wolves
  • Moose
  • Deer

and much more, this was already a pretty wild (vacation)! However, little did I know that it was going to be completely out of this world....

While hiking in a remote part of the park I encountered Richard Simmons!

Obviously, I was shocked to see him dashing through the park - especially with no one else around. At first I thought maybe he was exercising but as he zipped by I could tell something wasn't right.

He looked terrified!

I yelled after him, "Is everything okay?!"
"She's after me!" he shrieked. And then he was gone.

I had no idea what to think. I thought about running after him but he was moving way too fast. Plus I would probably just end up completely lost.

So I kept going.

It was only about 5 minutes later I saw someone else dashing along the path. At first I thought I was really going crazy because (and you'll never believe this) it was Martha Stewart!

And she was holding a huge frosted cupcake.

"What are you doing here?!" I exclaimed.
"Trying to force Simmons to eat a cupcake... he hasn't had a carb in 30 years and that needs to change!" she declared, not even slowing down.

And then she too disappeared.

You don't even want to know who I saw when I got back to town...
