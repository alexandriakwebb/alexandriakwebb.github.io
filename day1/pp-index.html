Puppy Problems

Welcome!

This site is for people who have a new puppy that isn't the most well behaved house guest.

On this site you'll find resources and a whole community to help you live in harmony with your puppy.

The goal of this community is to:

  • Help people understand their puppy.
  • Provide tips and tools to break bad habits - especially those that lead to serious problems in grown dogs such as food aggression.
  • Prevent puppies from being put up for adoption due to bad behaviors.

Rules

The biggest rule of the Puppy Problems community is to respect one another. This means no cursing or rude behavior. Other than that please:

  • Proofread comments before posting. This will ensure a good user experience for all.
  • Cite your sources. If you're offering tips please support your info with facts and whenever possible, links to research. This will allow others to make informed decisions.
  • Be active. This is a community. Please respond to comments on your posts and jump into the conversation whenever possible.
  • Be kind. Any suspected animal abuse will be reported immediately.
